Frequently Asked Questions

What are the definitive operating hours of Nzoli Medical Center?

We operate seamlessly 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, encompassing overnight emergency cases, standard weekend blocks, and statutory public holidays without suspension of care.

Do diagnostic ultrasound scans or X-ray procedures require prior appointment logs?

Emergency evaluations receive immediate, on-site priority. For standard elective diagnostics, logging an entry via this digital booking platform minimises baseline on-premises waiting intervals.

Where precisely can patients locate the main physical medical facility?

The clinic is established precisely at Plot 88 Bukoba Road, New Kumbu within Masaka, Uganda—positioned directly adjacent to Solo Hites Hotel for easy regional identification.

What special provisions exist for prospective maternal and prenatal care cases?

We coordinate dedicated clinical tracks containing scheduled antenatal checks, fetal monitoring sweeps, immunizations, and round-the-clock emergency obstetric coverage led by skilled midwifes and doctors.
